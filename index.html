PhiBERT – Enhanced PHI De-identification for Medical Document Security (for Ideas2IT)

PhiBERT is a cutting-edge Protected Health Information (PHI) de-identification system designed to align with HIPAA regulations. At its core, it leverages the power of BioBERT, a domain-specific adaptation of the BERT model pre-trained on extensive biomedical corpora. Our system was meticulously trained on a 'gold standard' dataset to accurately recognize and classify 18 different types of PHI as defined by HIPAA. It is now instrumental across the healthcare industry, seamlessly integrating into communication channels as a plugin to prevent the accidental disclosure of sensitive PHI, thus bolstering data security and patient privacy.
